How it works

From question to insight in seconds

No SQL. No BI tool access request. No waiting for a data pull. Ask like you'd ask a teammate who knows every Mentor.

1

Ask in plain English

Type your question naturally — about a district, a specific Mentor, a topic, a time range, or a sentiment. No special syntax needed.

2

Get answers from real community data

The Listener searches Mentor profiles, posts, and activity across the community — surfacing the relevant members, quotes, and patterns.

3

Use it in your work

Prep for a discovery call. Bring a story to an AE meeting. Find a Mentor who'd be a great reference. Spot a district ready for expansion.
